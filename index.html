Throughout my life, no matter where I found myself, there has always been one thing that's always drawn me in and pushed me towards my creative goals: compelling storytelling. After spending over 15 years in staff and business management leading and working with teams of various sizes, I’ve recently been continuing my education at Full Sail University, studying some age old passions of mine: Computer Animation & Game Development. During my free time, when not enjoying playing games myself, I usually have a computer in my lap writing fiction, studying web development (which is how I built this site from scratch!), and learning two of the most common video game engines: Unity and the Unreal Engine.

My current career objective is to join a team in the software or game development industry, working with passionate individuals with whom I can utilize both my leadership experience along with my passion for and knowledge of the video game industry. I am looking to build a relationship with a team and organization as I continue earning my Game Development bachelors, so once it is completed I will have the experience and a breadth of on-the-job knowledge so that I may easily move into a deeper role creating video games.
